Kollam District

Kollam, also known as Quilon, is a beautiful coastal city in the state of Kerala. Famous for its backwaters, beaches, and historical significance, Kollam is one of the oldest port cities in the Arabian Sea. It is a gateway to the Ashtamudi Lake and is known for its cashew processing, coir manufacturing, and marine activities.

Major attractions include Ashtamudi Lake, Kollam Beach, Jatayu Earth's Center, and Thangassery Light House.
