Trivandrum District

Thiruvananthapuram, also known as Trivandrum, is the capital city of Kerala. A vibrant mix of tradition and modernity, Trivandrum is home to ancient temples, palaces, museums, and beautiful beaches. It is also a major center for education, IT, and research institutions.

Major attractions include the Sree Padmanabhaswamy Temple, Kovalam Beach, Napier Museum, and Veli Tourist Village.
