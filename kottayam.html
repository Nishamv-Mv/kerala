Kottayam District

Kottayam is a beautiful land of letters, latex, and lakes. It is known for its high literacy rate, vibrant publishing industry, and rubber plantations. The district is surrounded by scenic backwaters, hills, and religious centers, making it a culturally rich destination.

Major attractions include Vembanad Lake, Ilaveezhapoonchira, Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary, and Thazhathangady Juma Masjid.
