Kerala Tourism

Alappuzha District

Alappuzha, also known as Alleppey, is often called the “Venice of the East” because of its picturesque backwaters, canals, and lagoons. Famous for houseboat cruises, paddy fields, and coir industry, Alappuzha is a top tourist destination in Kerala.

Major attractions include Alappuzha Backwaters, Alappuzha Beach, Kumarakom, Pathiramanal Island, and the famous Nehru Trophy Boat Race.
